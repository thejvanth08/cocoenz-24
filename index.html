COMPUTER AND COMMUNICATION ENGINEERING

Our department offers a platform for exploring cutting-edge areas like Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Cloud Computing, Computer Networks, Embedded Systems, Internet of Things, and advanced communication engineering intricacies. We're dedicated to equipping students with the knowledge to drive innovation in the ever-evolving world of technology.

ABOUT COCOENZ

COCOENZ'24 is a national-level techfest organized by the Department of Computer and Communication Engineering at Sri Sairam Institute of Technology on September 20, 2024. It celebrates innovation with four technical competitions: Triwizard Trivia (quiz), QuidUX Cup (UI/UX design), Wingardium Web-iosa (full stack development), and Hogwarts Innovation Fair (project expo). Non-technical events include Galleons & Gringotts (Monopoly), Chamber of Secrets (forensics), and The Snap of the Sorcerer (photography).
