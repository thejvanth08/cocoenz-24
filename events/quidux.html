"Every tap is like a spell, and every page a new adventure."


Welcome to the thrilling journey of the QUIDUX CUP! Get ready to showcase your creativity and problem-solving skills in this UI/UX Web Design competition. Whether you're using Figma, Adobe Illustrator, or any other design tool, your mission is to tackle a design challenge and create an innovative UI wireframe within a set time frame. The best designs will be evaluated on creativity, usability, and presentation, so bring your A-game and let your imagination flow!

Round Description

    In this competition, you'll be given a design challenge on the spot, and your task is to create a UI wireframe that addresses the challenge effectively. Your wireframe should include a basic layout and user flow, demonstrating a deep understanding of user needs and ensuring a seamless user experience.

    • Duration: 1 hour 30 minutes
    • Submission: Via a provided link

Rules & Regulations

  1. Bring Your Own Device: Participants must bring their own laptops, fully charged and ready to go.
  2. Set Up in Advance: Ensure your Figma/Adobe Illustrator or other design accounts are set up and accessible before the event begins.
  3. Originality Counts: While pre-existing templates are allowed, originality will be highly valued, so aim to create something fresh and unique.
  4. Time Management: Ensure that your design is submitted within the given time limit. Late submissions may not be accepted.
  5. Avoid Plagiarism: All designs must be original. Plagiarism will lead to immediate disqualification.
  6. No AI Tools: The use of AI tools to generate web pages or design elements is strictly prohibited. All work must be done manually
