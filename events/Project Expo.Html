Innovation Unleashed

"Dream big, build bold, inspire innovation."


Step into a world where creativity meets technology! In this electrifying event, participants will showcase their ground-breaking projects, each a testament to their innovative thinking and technical expertise. Whether it's cutting-edge software, revolutionary hardware, or an ingenious blend of both, this expo is the stage where ideas come to life. Witness the future as you explore a diverse array of projects, each pushing the boundaries of what is possible. Get ready to be inspired, amazed, and perhaps even challenged to think beyond the ordinary!

Rules & Regulations

  1. Projects can be individual or team-based (3).
  2. Registration must be completed by the specified deadline.
  3. Project presentation should include brief summary highlights and should focus on purpose, the process, and the results, giving a concise snapshot of the project's essence.
  4. Each team will have a specific time slot to present their project (5-6 minutes), followed by viva questions from the judge (2-3 minutes).
  5. A project presentation should include the following slides: Title slide, Problem statement, Abstract, Objective, Methodology, Demo video(if available) and conclusion.
  6. Innovation and Creativity, Technical Execution, Practical Application, and Presentation and Communication Skills will be awarded extra points
  7. Adherence to Guidelines Participants are responsible for bringing all necessary equipment, including laptops, cables, etc.
  8. The decisions of the event coordinators and judges are final and binding.

E-Certificates will be provided for all participants.
