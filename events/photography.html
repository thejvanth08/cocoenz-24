Photography

"Capture moments, create timeless stories"


Welcome to The**SNAP OF THE SORCERER**, where your photography skills and creativity will shine! Capture the essence of nature in our photography contest! Choose from theme Nature basis . Showcase your skills in capturing the beauty of the natural world, from the tiniest details to the grandest landscapes. Describe your photos beautifully to bring your vision to life. This event will challenge you in two exciting rounds, testing both your photography techniques and creative ideas. Each photo taken by the participants should have some meaning behind it, or they should provide a description of the thought or idea they had while capturing the photo.

Rules & Regulations For Photography

  1. The photo should not offend a person or place at any cause.
  2. The photo should follow the nature theme( with no water marks).
  3. The finalist will be selected based on the no of likes and judges final decision.
      4. Pictures of the product,properties..etc, are presented in the AV.
