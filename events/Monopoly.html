Welcome to the game “GALLIONS AND GRINGOTTS”, an exciting and competitive quiz event designed to test your knowledge of the business world. Whether you're a seasoned professional or just passionate about business, this event offers a thrilling opportunity to showcase your expertise and learn more about key concepts in buying, selling, and managing properties, along with other vital business topics.
  1. Round 1: DIAGON ALLEY

    Description:A traditional auction format where the duelling wizards(buyers) will buy an selling properties,products,...etc. The auction will focus on property management, and market strategies.Points will be given based on products they bought and also on balance amount they have.

  2. Round 2: PENSIEVE

    Description:In Round 2, the remaining contenders will face voiceover questions, testing their quick thinking and mastery of the magical world. Responses will be judged based on accuracy and swiftness, with only the sharpest minds advancing further in the competition.

Rules & Regulations

  1. Each team must consist of 3 members.
  2. Interdisciplinary teams are preferred, encouraging a diversity of skills and perspectives.
  3. Participants will be provided with some amount for auction.
  4. Pictures of the product,properties..etc, are presented in the AV.
  5. Buyers after buying all the selling products, we will give points based on the value of the products(profit or loss) and also with balance amount.
  6. Participants will be shortlisted based on those points.
  7. Shortlisted participants will proceed to the second round of the competition.
  8. Responses must be clear and accurate. Incorrect or unclear answers will not earn points.
  9. If a contestant answers incorrectly, other participants may stop them by saying,“LEVICORPUS” and answering correctly to earn the points.
  10. During a contestant’s turn, other participants must remain silent to avoid giving hints.

Prepare yourself for a fun and intellectually stimulating experience as you navigate through both rounds, testing your business acumen.

AWARD(BEST INVESTOR) WILL BE GIVEN FOR THE WINNER
