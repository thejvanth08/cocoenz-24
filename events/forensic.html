Chamber Of Secrets

"Seek truth, like Harry finds the Snitch!"


Welcome to the exciting journey of "FORENSIC" where you'll embark on a quest to prove your amazing skills! This event is divided into two thrilling rounds that will challenge your Thinking abilities and ignite mystery spirit.

Rounds Overview

  1. Round 1: The Wizarding Magistral

    Description:Imagine stepping into a realm of mystery with 20 questions that will puzzle and intrigue you. Your mission? To decipher the enigmatic questions and unveil the hidden answers. It's like solving riddles in the world of Secrets.

  2. Round 2: The Goblet Quest

    Description:Congratulations to the chosen ones! You've unlocked the second level – "The Goblet Quest." In this phase, you'll face SOME MYSTERY challenges. It's a chance to be the hero of your own INVESTIGATION adventure. Defeat the challenges, Find the CLUES like a sorcerer casting spells, and conquer the MYSTERY world.

Rules & Regulations

  1. Each team can consist of a maximum of 3 members.
  2. Interdisciplinary teams are preferred, encouraging a diversity of skills and perspectives.
  3. Participants will be provided with a question paper to answer the questions.
  4. For the shortlisted teams we’ll tell a story.
  5. Participants have to collect the hints from the story.
  6. Participants are required to complete the question paper within the allotted time frame.

E-Certificates will be provided for all participants.
